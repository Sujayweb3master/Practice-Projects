A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1931-2015


Biography

A. P. J. Abdul Kalam, full name Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman. He served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Known as the "People's President," Kalam was a key figure in India's missile development and space technology programs. He played a pivotal role in the development of India's first indigenous satellite launch vehicle and ballistic missile. Kalam was also a prolific writer and inspirational speaker, focusing on topics like education, youth empowerment, and national development. His contributions to science and his vision for a developed India have left a lasting legacy.
